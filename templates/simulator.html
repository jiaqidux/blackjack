Blackjack Strategy Simulator

This tool simulates thousands of blackjack hands to compare three playing strategies over time: the Optimal Strategy (following the strategy tables), the Dealer's Strategy (hitting below 17, standing otherwise), and a Random Strategy (choosing actions arbitrarily)

To evaluate their performance, the simulation tracks two main metrics:

  • Wins, loses, and draws, recorded on a per-round basis.
  • Net Units, to represent the running profit or loss measured in base bet units, showing long-term performance independent of bet size.

As expected, even the Optimal Strategy shows a slight long-term loss due to the house edge. However, it consistently outperforms the Dealer's and Random strategies, demonstrating that mathematically optimal play minimizes losses over time.
